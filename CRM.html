CRM: Customer Relationship Management

Loddepot CRM enables you to document your sales activities, aiming to boost sales by providing a platform to streamline processes, manage customer relationships, and track performance efficiently.

The CRM tool within LodDepot is especially useful when the business has multiple sales representatives. 

  1. Sales Representative Sort Tool
    You can sort the customer list by Sales Rep
  2. Search Tool
    Search function allows you to narrow down the list further. You can search by Name/Address/City/State
  3. Frequency
    Call frequency can be set for each customer individually. In combination with Last Order, Call Frequency and Last Activity CRM determines the next Due Day.
    You also have an option to select a preferred week day for a call 
  4. Due Day
    As mentioned above, the Due Day will be set automatically based on the Last Order date, Last Activity date and Frequency settings. When the Due Date is past due, it will be highlighted in red to immediately bring attention of a sales representative to the this customer. 
  5. Last Activity
    Last Activity pulls information from orders and sales activities that you create within the CRM. 

Details

  1. Within the CRM tool, you can quickly edit customers' information
  2. Send an SMS with one-click access to the web-store to the phone tied to the customer's account
  3. Send an Email with one-click access to the web-store to the phone tied to the customer's account

Activities

"Activities" is the main feature of the CRM tool. Here you can record and track progress on each of the clients. 

  • Click Add and choose one of the following Activities: Sales Call, Client Visit, Meeting, Email, Onboarding, Referral Request, Office Sale, Web Store Sale, Mobile Sale or Other. 
  • When creating a new activity, you must also select one of the results: Call Back, Follow Up, Postpone Order, Sale Order, Not Interested, Stop Following, Start Following or Other. 
  • You also have an option to set a Follow Up Date, which will result in a new Due Date being set.
  • Enter a comment to save more information regarding the sale activity
