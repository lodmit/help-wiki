You can use the links from Company Profile page to view your catalog and web store. However, if you need to send web-store access to a customer, please use the following steps. 

Navigate to Customer/Vendors → Select a customer → Click ‘More’ → Choose one of the options

4. One Click Access: a customer will have to follow the exact link that is sent in the invitation email or text to access the web-store. It does not require the customer to create a login and a password.

5. Web Store Invitation: a customer will have to follow the link that is sent in the invitation email to create an account. The customer must remember the login and password.
